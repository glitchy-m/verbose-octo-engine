screen audio visualizer

share a tab or your screen, allow audio, then pick a visualizer. works in chromium-based browsers that support "share tab audio" (check the "share audio" box when choosing a tab).

how to get tab audio
  1. click "share audio".
  2. choose "tab" in the picker, check "share audio", then click share.
  3. play audio in that tab, the visualizer will respond.
built-in visualizers: bars, radial, waveform, particles, spectrogram. plus classic oscilloscope (preexisting style). enjoy!! ;)
